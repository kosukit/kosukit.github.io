Global Decline in Birth Rates

Recently, the world has experienced a general decline in birth rates, a trend that has been notable in many countries around the globe. This trend has been especially notable in developed countries, which face a challenging task to resolve the issue. Japan, in particular, is well-known for this drop due to it being among the first to experience the phenomenon, which poses challenges for its economy and society.

This narrative visualization will explore this global trend of declining birth rates with a focus on Japan's situation, which may provide key insight into what the future may pose for other advanced economies.
